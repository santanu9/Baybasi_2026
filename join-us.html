Ways to Join

Choose the path that fits you

Member

Why: access events, participate in programs, elect the Board, join leadership teams, and help decide Baybasi’s future.

Criteria: align with Baybasi’s purpose, participate in programs, and pay minimum donation amount set by the Board.

Volunteer

Why: help the community, give back to society, earn volunteer points, and redeem rewards in future.

Criteria: align with Baybasi’s purpose, participate in events, and agree to Baybasi terms.

Subscriber / Attend

Enjoy a homely environment, quality programs, Bengali food, family-friendly events, and discounts for students, seniors, and families.

Future options may include annual subscriptions, event subscriptions, day passes, and value packs.

Sponsor

Support Baybasi while gaining brand visibility, audience access, goodwill, and direct engagement opportunities.
