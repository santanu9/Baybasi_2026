Why Sponsor Baybasi

Visibility with goodwill

Baybasi offers sponsors access to a highly engaged Bay Area community through major cultural events, festivals, publications, media, and community programs.

Brand Visibility

Showcase your brand across events, sponsor areas, website sections, and publications.

Affluent Audience

Connect with a high-value community audience across the San Francisco Bay Area.

Goodwill

Direct Engagement

Opportunities for lead generation and relationship-building at signature events.
