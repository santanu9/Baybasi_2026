About Us

Rooted in heritage. Growing through service.

Baybasi serves the San Francisco Bay Area through Bengali and Indian cultural programs, community enrichment, and charitable initiatives.

Our Story

From a small gathering to a Bay Area nonprofit community

What began in 2000 as a small community group in Foster City among Bengali friends and their families evolved into an Indian nonprofit charitable organization in 2008. Today, Baybasi serves communities throughout the SF Bay Area with a deep commitment to cultural heritage, programs, service, and giving back.

Our Purpose

Four pillars of Baybasi

Indian Heritage

Promote awareness of Indian ethnic identity and its cultural and spiritual heritage.

Bengali and Indian Culture

Organize cultural events involving Bengali and other Indian traditional and contemporary songs, drama, and dances.

Member Enrichment

Plan and host conferences, workshops, seminars, and meetings to help members learn, network, and share experiences.

Philanthropy

Organize events to raise funds for relief, charitable causes, and community service.
